What does HappyNest expect from a Nester?

What we expect from you

Respect the rules of the owner for the pet

● Honesty at all times! It also means that if you are not comfortable with a pet, you can tell us
● Regular updates in the group : pictures, videos, messages when you arrive and when you leave
● Always talk in the group so HappyNest can follow up and have your back in case of misunderstanding

And of course...to cover the basics for the pets

● Clean the litter box or accidents
● Walk the dog on the leash at all time, be careful when approaching other
dogs
● Clean and refill water and food
● Play, cuddle, enjoy !

What we will never ask you

● You are not the housekeeper. You can help with small things from time to time if asked, but if you are not comfortable with an owner request, feel free to let us know.
● To do a booking you don’t want to

How to get ready to take care of a pet?

Boarding / House sitting

● Make sure your home is safe for the pet (windows closed, balcony safe, etc)
● Remove anything you care about to avoid the pet to break it
● Set your own rules for your apt (where the pet is allowed, etc), specially if
you live in a shared apartment
● For house sitting, take what you need to make yourself comfortable

Dropin visit / dog walking

● Make sure to know where is everything you’ll need for the pet (refer to meet and greet list)
● Make sure you have all the access you need (keys, card, etc.)
