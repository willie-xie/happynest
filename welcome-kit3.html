How to create a routine?

"I try my best to give them food at the same time everyday. I think this helps the pet feel more comfortable and sets a routine"

"First I ask what is the owner's routine, then adapt it according to me"

"I create my own with the dog, to include cuddles, play time and walks"

How to keep the owner updated?

"I send lots of photos, videos and information to the pet owner, to show them that their pet is happy and safe. Give them the peace of mind knowing their pet is being taken care of"

"I try to update the owner everytime I interact with the pet, or if it does something unique/cute/funny. But first ask the owner what type of update the owner prefers"

"I send pictures once a day and I am doing my best to be always reactive when the owner connects with me"

HappyNest tips : even for a more difficult news, try to always keep things positive. (ex : Coco had a little accident today, but I cleaned it and changed the litter again so it doesn’t happen anymore)


Small details pet owners love

"I think pet owners love to see their pet happy. I love taking lovely photos and also take photos/videos when pets are sleeping, playing or doing something fun"

"My owners always like me to connect with them, tell them how the day went and what we have been doing"

​HappyNest tips : owners love to have news about their pets, but they also like to know they are in a nice environment. Time to time you can send a picture of their bowls, or litter, or anything that shows they are well taking care of. 

Emergency and guidelines

Stay calm and ask for help
In any stressful situation you might have with the pet you are taking care of, you have to contact HappyNest as soon as possible and give details of what happened, then we will inform the pet's parents and communicate on how we are solving the problem.

● The cat disappeared
If you saw where he went, don’t run after him, it will scare him more. Try to gently follow and call him, maybe using food. If you don’t know where he might be, please contact HappyNest immediately !

● The dog run away
First you need to try and follow the dog. If you lose sight you can go to the police station and ask to see the CCTV to try and check. In the meantime, contact HappyNest

● The pet is injured
Use the vet contact you got during the meet and greet, and if the vet is closed or too far away, you have to check the nearest pet hospital to take the pet (HappyNest can help you, feel free to contact us)

● The pet is sick
First you need to identify symptoms and for how long the pet has been sick. You can talk in the group with the owner to see if that happened before. And then you can take actions : go to the vet, or follow instructions we will give you

● The pet is feeling down
Try to identify for how long the pet has been like this and make sure the pet is drinking and eating properly. If not, try to spend more time with the pet. If the pet is still unhappy, it could be because they are missing their parents or are sick, contact Happynest and ask in the group with the pet's parents for tips.
